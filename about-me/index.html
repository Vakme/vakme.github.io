About me

I’m a Lead Frontend Developer (Angular/React/Vue) based in Kraków, Poland. I’m fond of clouds - I work with Azure and AWS, and I also host personal projects on GCP. In my free time, I play games (mostly strategy games) and volunteer with the Polish Endometriosis Foundation.
